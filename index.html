How SPATYA Works

One app. Two experiences.

SPATYA is a dual-sided portal. The remodeler manages projects and earns from the marketplace. The homeowner sees a branded experience — and never knows SPATYA exists.

Mike manages everything from one dashboard

1

Sets up milestones — defines project phases, uploads job site photos, updates real-time progress.

2

Curates the catalog — uploads brands and products. Marks categories as constrained (needs his approval) or free (client picks alone).

3

Generates AI previews — photographs the client's space, selects materials, runs the AI render. Shares multiple options through the portal.

4

Gets paid automatically — at every milestone approval, payment triggers via Stripe Connect. Digital signature + timestamp. Money goes straight to his account.

5

Earns from every selection — brand deals, commissions, partnership slots. 100% to Mike. SPATYA takes zero commission.

Mrs. Johnson opens the portal and sees Mike's brand

1

Sees project progress — milestones, job site photos, real-time status. No need to call or text.

2

Browses materials — from Mike's curated catalog. Compares options, reads specs, saves favorites.

3

Previews in her own space — sees materials rendered in HER actual kitchen. Multiple options, multiple surfaces. Chooses with her eyes.

4

Approves and pays — digital signature on each milestone. Payment goes through automatically. Timestamped, traceable, legal-grade.

5

Picks extras freely — lamps, decor, accessories? She browses, selects, and checks out on her own. No waiting for approval on free categories.

Same app. Same data. Two interfaces. The client sees Mike's brand — never SPATYA.

Smart Control

Material Catalog & Category Logic

One boolean flag per category controls three behaviors: milestone visibility, checkout flow, and notification type. Constrained = remodeler approves. Free = client decides.

Full control where it matters. Autonomy where it doesn't. One flag, three behaviors.
